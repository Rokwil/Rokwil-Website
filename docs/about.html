About Rokwil

Pioneering property development in KwaZulu-Natal

Our Story

Rokwil Property Development, established in 2021 under the leadership of Roderick Robert Stainton, has rapidly become a transformative force in KwaZulu-Natal's property landscape. Based in Kloof, we are committed to excellence, innovation, and creating developments that not only meet the highest standards of quality but also contribute meaningfully to the communities we serve.

Our portfolio includes landmark projects such as Keystone Park, a massive 152-hectare logistics and industrial precinct with a R6.5 billion investment that has already created 2,000 permanent jobs in its first phase. Upon completion, this development is expected to generate approximately 6,500 permanent employment opportunities, making it one of the most significant economic drivers in the region.

We also developed Judges Court, a premium corporate office space that sets new standards for commercial development in the Upper Highway area. Our commitment to quality design, strategic positioning, and community impact has established Rokwil as a trusted leader in property development throughout KwaZulu-Natal.

Our Mission: To create world-class property developments that drive economic growth, create employment opportunities, and enhance the quality of life in KwaZulu-Natal and beyond.

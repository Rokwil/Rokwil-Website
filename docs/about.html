Our Story

Building the Future of KZN

Rokwil is a property development company based in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. Established in 2007 under the leadership of Rod Stainton, a chartered accountant with over 20 years' experience in property development, consulting, and management. Our vision is to act as a catalyst for economic change in the areas in which we operate.

We focus on large-format industrial and logistics projects along the Durban–Pietermaritzburg N3 corridor, with Keystone Park at Hammarsdale as our flagship development. This 152-hectare serviced logistics precinct yields over 1 million m² of platform area and has reached maturity with major blue-chip occupiers including Mr Price Group (100,000 m² under roof), Ackermans (94,000 m²), Pepkor (122,000 m²), plus manufacturing facilities for Dromex, Kerry Ingredients, Nu Dawn Eggs, Malda Pack, and many others.

Our developments are recognised by industry bodies including the Hammarsdale Cato Ridge Development Association and municipal planning authorities as catalytic projects for Outer-West KZN. We collaborate closely with local community leadership structures to support grassroots development and have strong relationships with regional and provincial government entities.

Our Mission

To create world-class property developments that drive economic growth, create employment opportunities, and enhance the quality of life in KwaZulu-Natal and beyond.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our growth

1

2005 – Foundations & Market Research

Early research begins on the economic potential of the N3 corridor. The founding team identifies a massive gap in industrial development between Durban and Pietermaritzburg.

2

2007 – Rokwil Formally Established

The business is officially registered with a core focus on industrial platforms, logistics hubs, and strategically located commercial zones.

3

2010 – First Infrastructure Collaborations

Rokwil starts working with civil engineering partners to design bulk services, stormwater networks, and future platform layouts for large-scale distribution sites.

4

2012 – Strategic Land Banking

Multiple parcels of land are acquired along key growth nodes, shaping the blueprint for future mega-developments.

5

2015 – First Industrial Park Completed

Rokwil delivers its first medium-scale industrial park, attracting logistics, manufacturing, and storage tenants. This proves the company's long-term viability in the region.

6

2017 – Partnerships With National Retailers

Major South African retail chains begin exploring DC opportunities with Rokwil, setting the stage for larger developments.

7

2019 – Engineering & Environmental Master Planning

Rokwil invests in environmental studies, road upgrades, zoning processes, and long-term master plans for a multi-phase industrial precinct.

8

2020 – Keystone Park Vision Activated

Bulk earthworks, road infrastructure, water reticulation, and platform preparation begin for what will become one of KZN's most strategically placed industrial precincts.

9

2021 – Tenant Commitments & Site Growth

Confirmed interest from logistics operators, FMCG suppliers, national retailers, and manufacturing partners drives rapid expansion.

10

2022 – Mega Distribution Centres Break Ground

The first large-format DCs are launched, involving tens of thousands of square metres of warehouse and handling facilities.

11

2023 – Community & Economic Impact Focus

Investments are made in access road upgrades, local contractor participation, skills development programs, environmental sustainability, and safer transport flows along the N3.

12

2024 – Portfolio Expansion & Regional Influence

Rokwil becomes recognized as a key role-player in the economic upliftment of Hammarsdale, Cato Ridge, and surrounding zones.

13

2025 – Innovation, Technology & New Horizons

Smart infrastructure, greener building solutions, and digital monitoring become part of the next generation of developments. Rokwil prepares new nodes, new tenants, and new land parcels for future phases.

Cookie & Privacy Policy

Understanding how we use cookies and local storage

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Rokwil Property Development, we are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we collect and use data on our website. This page explains our use of cookies and local storage without requiring you to accept cookies to view it.

What Are Cookies and Local Storage?

Cookies and local storage are small pieces of data stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How We Use Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to enhance your experience:

  • Theme Preference: We store your light/dark mode preference so the website remembers your choice on future visits.
  • WIP Disclaimer: We remember if you've acknowledged our website under construction notice so you don't see it repeatedly.
  • Cookie Consent: We store your cookie consent preference to remember your choice.

Firebase Services

Our website uses Google Firebase to manage and deliver content:

  • Firestore Database: Stores website content (text, images, project information). This is a read-only database that does not collect personal information.
  • Firebase Hosting: Our website is hosted on Firebase infrastructure for fast, reliable delivery.
  • No User Data Collection: Firebase is used solely for content delivery. We do not store personal information in Firebase databases.

Contact Form Data

When you submit our contact form, your information is processed through Static Forms, a third-party service. This data is sent directly to our email address (rod@rokwil.com) and is not stored on our website servers.

What we collect: Name, email address, and message content.

How we use it: To respond to your inquiries and provide information about our services.

Data Security

We take data security seriously. All data transmission is encrypted, and we do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process contact form submissions.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time through your browser settings
  • Disable local storage in your browser (though this may affect website functionality)
  • Request information about data we may have collected
  • Request deletion of your data

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Provides hosting, database, and content delivery services. Firebase's privacy policy applies. No personal data is stored in Firebase databases.
  • Static Forms: Processes contact form submissions. Their privacy policy applies to data submitted through forms.
  • Bootstrap Icons: Icon font library loaded from a CDN. No personal data is collected.
  • Google Fonts: Font library loaded from Google's servers. Google may collect usage statistics per their privacy policy.

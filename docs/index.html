What We Do

Transforming landscapes through strategic property development

Industrial Development

State-of-the-art logistics and warehousing facilities designed for modern business needs. Our 152-hectare Keystone Park development yields over 1 million m² of platform area and hosts mega DCs including Mr Price (100,000 m² under roof), Ackermans (94,000 m²), Pepkor (122,000 m²), plus manufacturing facilities for Dromex, Kerry Ingredients, Nu Dawn Eggs, Malda Pack, and many others.

Commercial Spaces

Premium corporate office spaces in prime locations with exceptional design and quality. Judges Court in Upper Highway offers businesses prestigious addresses with modern facilities.

Community Impact

Committed to creating developments that generate employment opportunities. With strong community engagement, we've created the Hammarsdale Steering Committee, placed 500+ locals in jobs, and founded the Victims of Crime Centre for support services in Hammarsdale.

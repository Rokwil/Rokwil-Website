Keystone Park

Hammarsdale Interchange, N3 Highway 152 Hectares R6.5 Billion Investment Industrial & Logistics

Keystone Park represents one of the most significant property developments in KwaZulu-Natal. This expansive 152-hectare logistics, warehousing, and light industrial precinct is strategically located at the Hammarsdale Interchange on the N3 highway, adjacent to one of South Africa's busiest transportation corridors.

With a total investment of R6.5 billion, this development has already created approximately 2,000 permanent jobs in its first phase. Upon completion, Keystone Park is expected to generate around 3,500 temporary construction jobs at peak and approximately 6,500 permanent employment opportunities, positioning itself as a key economic driver for the region.

Judges Court

Upper Highway Area, Durban Corporate Offices Premium Quality

Judges Court stands as a testament to Rokwil's commitment to quality design and prime location. The development emphasizes exceptional design quality, strategic positioning, and attention to detail that sets it apart in the commercial property market. Judges Court provides businesses with a prestigious address and modern facilities that support productivity and success.
